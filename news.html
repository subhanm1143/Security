NEW YORK GRANDMOTHER TRICKS SCAMMER

A Long Island, New York grandmother successfully tricked a scammer attempting to defraud her out of thousands of dollars. Jean Ebbert, a 73-year-old former 911 dispatcher, received a suspicious call from a man claiming to be her grandson in jail, requesting $8,000 for bail. Recognizing the scam, she played along, alerting authorities who later caught the suspect at her home. Security experts say this serves as a reminder to always verify suspicious calls before taking action.

ATTEMPTED BREAK-IN FOILED BY HOME SECURITY CAMERAS

A security camera system in Peachland, B.C., helped prevent a potential burglary when homeowners were alerted to an unknown individual lurking around their property.
